Our Complete Kitchen Ranges

Discover the style that defines your home and create the perfect living space. From classic craftsmanship to cutting-edge minimalism, we design for every taste, budget & style.

The Shaker Collections

The Shaker is often called the "little black dress"of kitchen design because of its perfect balance of form and function. This style originated with the 18th-century Shaker community, who valued utility, durability, and simplicity above all else.

Our Shaker ranges focuses on these core principles, delivering a kitchen that is incredibly versatile and guaranteed to stand the test of time.

Key Characteristics:

  • Clean Lines: Defined by a simple, framed door with a flat central panel, Shaker creates an uncluttered, calm, and spacious feel.
  • Versatility: Its simple geometry works seamlessly with both Traditional (using period handles and deep colours) and **Modern** (using sleek, metallic bar handles and minimalist finishes) designs.
  • Timelessness: Shaker is a design classic, ensuring your investment will not look dated in a few years.

The Modern Collections

Our Modern Collections focuses on creating seamless, minimalist spaces that prioritize clean design and cutting-edge functionality. These kitchens are defined by flat, high-gloss or matte doors and the distinct lack of visible handles (handleless design).

If you seek sleek lines, intelligent storage solutions, and a truly uncluttered aesthetic, the Modern range is the ultimate statement in contemporary living.

Design Highlights:

  • Handleless Doors: Creates a seamless, easy-to-clean facade.
  • Handleless Doors Options: Uses a spring action mechanism for a push to open action providing a hyper minimalist finish.
  • Handleless Doors Alternative: Uses a rail system where the top edge of the door or drawer is slightly shorter than the cabinet. The rail provies an ergonomic grip to open the door or drawer.
  • Integrated Appliances: Everything is hidden away for a cohesive look.
  • Bold Materials: Utilizes quartz, glass, concrete-effect finishes, and striking metal accents.

The Traditional Collections

The Traditional Collections embodies classic charm, warmth, and enduring quality. These ranges features more detailed cabinetry, often incorporating features like raised panels, ornate cornices, and elegant pilasters.

Perfect for homes that value intricate details and a sense of timeless craftsmanship, our Traditional kitchens create a welcoming, beautiful heart of the home.

Features & Craftsmanship:

  • Raised Panel Doors: Adds depth and a sense of luxury to the cabinetry.
  • Feature Display Units: Glass-fronted cabinets and plate racks for showcasing items.
  • Classic Finishes: Uses hand-painted effects, solid wood, and warm, inviting color palettes.
