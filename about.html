Crafting Beautiful Kitchens Since 2008

We are an English-managed company based in Spain, dedicated to creating bespoke, high-quality kitchens built around your life and style.

Over 15 Years of Trusted Kitchen Expertise

Designing, supplying, and installing dream kitchens for the Almería expat community.

Founded specifically to serve the international community in Almería, Artizan Kitchens understands the unique needs and expectations of our clients. We combine the reliability and quality standards you expect from a premium European brand with the local knowledge required for flawless installation in Spain. We manage the entire project from start to finish, allowing you to relax and enjoy the transformation of your home.
